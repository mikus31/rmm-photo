About Us

Dressed is a regionally-inspired gift shop specializing in t-shirts. The name of the shop is a play on words. Dressed as in po-boys (for the unfamiliar, see here) as in southeast Louisiana.

Hence the "regionally-inspired" bit above. A lot of our stuff will be Louisiana-related, but not all of it.

Starting a t-shirt shop is not a unique idea, but our ideas for t-shirts and our designs will be unique. Shop inspirations:

  • Homage
  • Stately Type
  • Dirty Coast

I've always been a big fan of place-related design, and think people enjoy wearing clothing that represents the places they love. Whether it's home or some far-off land they visited once upon a time.

So that's what we're going to make. Great designs and great shirts.
